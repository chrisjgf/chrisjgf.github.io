Released in 2017, I developed this app in my spare-time at university to help change my spending habits. [Available here](https://apps.apple.com/gb/app/budgie/id1122060396)

> Managing your finances shouldn't be hard. With Budgie, keeping track of what you spend is no longer an issue. At a glance, view, sort and tag your weekly expenditure. Start tracking today, save money for tomorrow.
