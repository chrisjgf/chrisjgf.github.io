I designed and developed this app for the University of York in Summer 2016. The app was developed in Ionic due to client budget and speed requirements. [Available here](https://itunes.apple.com/gb/app/chemistry-york/id1155539697?mt=8)
 
> Links and information for new/prospective undergraduates studying Chemistry at the University of York. Includes maps, tours, interview guides, and more.
